Why It Matters

Small household habits can create a huge environmental impact.

The Impact of Household Waste

Household waste affects our environment, health, and future. Many daily habits increase pollution without people realising it. By making smarter choices, we can help create a cleaner and more sustainable community.

🌍

Environment

Waste pollutes land and water and contributes to climate change.

💊

Health

Dirty surroundings and unmanaged waste can spread diseases.

🏙️

Cities

Overflowing landfills make cities look unclean and unsafe.

1

Mixing Waste

Throwing all waste together makes recycling difficult.

2

Overusing Plastic

Single-use plastics can increase pollution and effect the environment negatively.

Common Bad Habits
3

Food Waste

Wasting food increases landfill waste and wasted resources.

4

Not Recycling

Recyclable materials are often thrown away unnecessarily which creates pollution.

5

Burning Waste

Burning waste releases harmful gases into the air and reduces air quality which is bad for health.

What You Can Do

Small actions can create a cleaner and healthier future.

How You Can Help

Reduce

Buy only what you need and avoid wasting products.

Reuse

Reuse bags, bottles, and containers whenever possible.

Recycle

Separate household waste properly before throwing it away.

Simple Everyday Tips

  • Use reusable water bottles
  • Avoid single-use plastics
  • Store food properly to reduce waste
  • Teach family members about recycling
