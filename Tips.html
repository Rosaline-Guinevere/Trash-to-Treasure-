Food Storing Tips

Store food smartly to reduce waste, save money, and keep food fresh longer.

Why Food Storage Matters

Improper food storage leads to spoilage, wasted money, and more trash in landfills. Good storage helps reduce food waste and keeps food fresh for longer.

Practical Tips

Airtight Containers

Store dry foods like rice and flour safely.

Label Food

Write dates on leftovers to avoid waste.

Freeze Food

Keep meat and bread in the freezer for longer life.

Separate Fruits

Some fruits make others ripen faster.

Plan Meals

Buy only what you need each week.

Use Glass Jars

Better than single-use plastic.

Everyday Habits

  • Keep a “use first” box in the fridge
  • Cook smaller portions
  • Share extra food with others
  • Teach children about food waste

Trash to Treasure

Proper household waste management helps reduce pollution, protect nature, and create a cleaner and healthier future for our community.
