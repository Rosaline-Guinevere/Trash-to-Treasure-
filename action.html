Take Action for a Cleaner Future

Simple actions like reducing, reusing, and recycling household waste can help create cleaner and more sustainable communities.

Reduce Waste at Home

Simple habits like reusing containers, separating rubbish, and reducing plastic use can help families create cleaner and more sustainable communities.

Recycle Properly

Choose reusable bags, bottles, and containers instead of single-use plastics.

Compost Food Waste

Keep Communities Clean

Throw rubbish into bins properly and encourage others to keep streets and waterways clean.

Reduce Food Waste

Plan your meals carefully and store food properly to reduce the amount of leftovers and expired food thrown away every day.

Use Recycling Bins

Use separate recycling bins for plastic, paper, glass, and metal so waste can be managed and recycled more effectively.

Donate Unused Items

Donate clothes, books, toys, and other reusable items to people in need instead of throwing them into the rubbish.

Join Clean-Up Activities

Take part in community clean-up activities to help keep streets, parks, rivers, and neighborhoods cleaner and healthier for everyone.
